AN EXPLOSION

OF NATURAL GOODNESS

When the forces of nature and homemade goodness collided...

Super 'Nola Natural was created through an earnest desire to jumpstart my day with a breakfast that was high in fiber and protein, contained no refined sugar and was chock full of whole foods, real foods, natural foods, super foods!

We take pride in that fact that our products are made in Greenville, South Carolina, using as many locally sourced ingredients as possible. We are certified through the South Carolina Department of Agriculture as a SC Product. We are also a member of the South Carolina Specialty Food Association.
